Intro

I am a data scientist specializing in Data Visualization, NLP, andData Warehousing. I graduated MS in Data Analytic from Northeastern University. Most of my time right now is researching alpha to improve my own trading algorithm, hopefully to be funded one day.

I am a big fan of languages and have self studied Japanese, Chinese and French on Duolingo for 3 years. One time on the flight to Tokyo, i talked with flight attendants in Japanese. But then it is quite embarrased when they started responding with more advanced dialogues. Therefore, I promise myself one day I will become a polyglot, engaging in any conversations confidently with the help of NLP.

In terms of working experience, I had internship at 2 hospitality conglomerates in Vietnam. Since then, I have started a love for this interesting industry, which requires generosity and detail-attention. I was also a Teaching Assistant for Data Warehousing and SQL class at Northeatern. Honestly, that was the best moment of my life when I got to know lots of like-minded people.
