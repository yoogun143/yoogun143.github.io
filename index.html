Intro

✅Work Experience

I am a Data Analyst at Viettel Telecom specializing in Data Visualization, Data Mining, and Machine Learning. My main task is to:

No. Description
1 Routinely keep track, investigate cause of falling KPIs and propose immediate action.
2 Run predictive modeling to find customer segments for campaigns.
3 Solve Adhoc case studies from BOD.
4 Coop with Data Engineer to build new report and dashboard for management.
5 Assess new data solutions from outsource partners.

Before that, I worked as Project Coordinator for 2 years at Vicostone Dallas, where I managed flow of goods manufacturing in Vietnam ➽ shipping to US by sea ➽ distributing to customers.

I was also a Teaching Assistant for Data Warehousing and SQL class at Northeatern University. Honestly, that was the best moment of my life when I got to know lots of like-minded people.

✅Education

I graduated B.S. in Corporate Finance at Nationals Economics University, Hanoi and finished my M.S. in Data Analytics from Northeastern University (Boston,US). (Interestingly, both university abbreviations are NEU😘)

✅Interests

Data is a my breathe and smile. I am obsessed with my own personal data ranging from amount of money I spent on which segments everyday, to consistency of how I work out every week, etc,..

I read Tarot for my friends when they need emotional healing advice and I am now exploring astrology in order to communicate with my spirit guides . I am intrigued by the unknown of the world.

I am also a big fan of languages and have self studied Japanese, Chinese and French on Duolingo for 3 years. One time on the flight to Tokyo, I talked with flight attendants in Japanese. But then it is quite embarrased when they started responding with more advanced dialogues. Therefore, I promise myself one day I will become a polyglot.
